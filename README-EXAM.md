# ICT Mid-Term Examination System

## 📚 Overview
This is a comprehensive ICT examination system for Primary School Grades 1-5. Students can login, take their grade-appropriate exam, and receive detailed results with printable reports.

## ✨ Features

### For Students:
- ✅ Login with student name
- ✅ Select grade level (Grade 1-5)
- ✅ Take exam with three sections:
  - **Section A: Objectives** (Multiple Choice)
  - **Section B: Fill in the Blanks**
  - **Section C: Theory Questions**
- ✅ Instant feedback on answers
- ✅ Detailed results with grading
- ✅ Printable report cards

### Exam Structure:

#### Grade 1 & 2 (25 Marks Total)
- Section A: 15 Marks (15 questions)
- Section B: 5 Marks (5 questions)
- Section C: 5 Marks (5 questions)

#### Grade 3 (25 Marks Total)
- Section A: 15 Marks (15 questions)
- Section B: 5 Marks (5 questions)
- Section C: 5 Marks (5 questions)

#### Grade 4 & 5 (30 Marks Total)
- Section A: 20 Marks (20 questions)
- Section B: 5 Marks (5 questions)
- Section C: 5 Marks (5 questions)

## 🚀 How to Use

Note: The application root (`/`) now serves the public portal landing — open `http://localhost:5510/` to reach the portal (the Login page is at `/login.html`).

### Option 1: Use New Exam System
1. Open `index-exam.html` in your web browser
2. Enter student's full name
3. Select the appropriate grade level
4. Click "Start Examination"
5. Answer all questions in the three sections
6. View detailed results
7. Print the result report if needed

### Option 2: Use Original Quiz System
1. Open `index.html` in your web browser for the original interactive quiz

## 🖨️ Printing Results

The system includes a professional printable report format with:
- Student information
- Detailed section-by-section performance
- Overall score and percentage
- Grade and remarks
- Teacher and parent signature sections

To print:
1. Complete the exam
2. Click "Print Results" button on the results screen
3. Use your browser's print dialog (Ctrl+P or Cmd+P)

## 💾 Data Storage

Results are automatically saved in the browser's local storage, allowing students and teachers to review past exam attempts.
